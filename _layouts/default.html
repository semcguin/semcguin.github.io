California Public School Construction Spending

This project looks at a never-before-analyzed data set of California public K-12 construction since 1994. Reveals spending trends across construction types and racial demographics.

Crime and Food Trucks in San Francisco

Do food trucks correspond with increased noise violations? What are the most dangerous times and places to be in San Francisco? This and more answered here using SQL to access a database of crime, noise violations, and food trucks in San Francisco.

Here I used the API for college scorecard to extract information on student debt and college tuition. I analyzed this data to determine the relationships between student debt, poverty, and tuition costs.
